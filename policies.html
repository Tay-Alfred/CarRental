**Car Rental Terms and Policies**

Welcome Duke's Car Rentals! Before proceeding with your car rental reservation, please take a moment to review our terms and policies outlined below: **1. Booking and Reservation:** - All reservations must be made by individuals aged 18 and above. - Reservation changes or cancellations should be made at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled pickup time. - We reserve the right to cancel any reservation due to unforeseen circumstances or non-compliance with our terms. **2. Rental Requirements:** - A valid driver's license issued by the renter's country of residence must be presented at the time of pickup. - Additional drivers must be listed on the rental agreement and meet our age and license requirements. - Renters under the age of 25 may be subject to a young driver surcharge. **3. Payment and Charges:** - Payment is required at the time of booking or pickup, depending on the reservation type. - Accepted payment methods include credit/debit cards and cash deposits. - Additional charges may apply for optional services such as insurance, fuel, and equipment rental. - Any fines, tolls, or traffic violations incurred during the rental period are the responsibility of the renter. **4. Insurance Coverage:** - Our rental vehicles come with basic insurance coverage included. - Additional insurance options, such as Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) and Theft Protection (TP), are available for purchase. - Please review the terms of your insurance coverage carefully to understand your liabilities and limitations. **5. Vehicle Usage and Restrictions:** - The rented vehicle should only be driven by authorized drivers listed on the rental agreement. - Use of the vehicle for illegal activities, racing, or off-road driving is strictly prohibited. - Smoking and transporting pets may result in cleaning fees. - The vehicle should be returned in the same condition as it was rented, with normal wear and tear expected. **6. Fuel Policy:** - Vehicles are typically provided with a full tank of fuel at the time of pickup. - Renters have the option to prepay for a full tank of fuel or return the vehicle with the same level of fuel as at the start of the rental. - Refueling charges may apply if the vehicle is returned with less fuel than initially provided. **7. Maintenance and Breakdowns:** - We maintain our rental fleet to the highest standards; however, in the event of a breakdown, renters should contact our 24/7 roadside assistance immediately. - Repairs or replacements necessary due to negligence or misuse by the renter may result in additional charges. **8. Vehicle Return:** - The vehicle must be returned to the designated rental location at the agreed-upon date and time. - Late returns may incur additional fees. - Renters should remove all personal belongings and trash from the vehicle before returning it. **9. Cancellation and Refund Policy:** - Cancellations made within the specified timeframe are eligible for a full or partial refund, depending on the terms of the reservation. - Refunds will be processed using the original payment method. **10. Dispute Resolution:** - Any disputes regarding rental charges, damages, or other issues should be reported to our customer service team for resolution. By proceeding with your reservation, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to abide by these terms and policies. If you have any questions or require clarification, please don't hesitate to contact us. We appreciate your business and hope you have a pleasant rental experience with us!
